### B2.1-001: Qt6 vs Qt 6

- **Status:** Confirmed
- **Severity:** Medium
- **Location:** 10+ files
- **Authority:** Qt brand style guide uses "Qt 6" (with space)
- **Evidence:**
  - `Qt6` (no space): architecture/Overview.md:202, build/BuildOptions.md:25,28, gui/README.md:3, internal/AGENTS.md:46,89,401
  - `Qt 6` (with space): user-guide/Studio.md:13, reference/ThirdPartyLicenses.md:8, getting-started/QuickStart.md:13, build/Build.md:48, gui/README.md:18, build/PlatformSupport.md:41,71,116
  - Mixed in same file: gui/README.md (line 3 uses `Qt6`, line 18 uses `Qt 6`)
- **Recommendation:** Standardize to `Qt 6` (with space) everywhere, matching the Qt brand style guide.
- **Target B3 Patch:** Patch 1
